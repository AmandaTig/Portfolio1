Welcome to Dog Buddies


We are a community of dog lovers who have access to the amazing dog park facility here in Carrickmacross, Co Monaghan. We have many events which are listed on our events section of the website, You are welcome to walk your dog or organise a playdate anytime with our members and use the dog walking facility during our open hours, Please ensure to clean up after your dog and respect other dogs and dog owners while using the facility.
