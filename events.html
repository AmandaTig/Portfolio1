Dog Meet Up

Dog Meet up happens every day at 10am, 2pm and 7pm. All dogs must be kept on lead and the walk is around the dog walk section. this is to encourage our furry friends to socialise with other dogs to either enable them to use the dog park off lead or just help their owner make new friends with other like minded dog lovers. Everyone is welcome.

Dog Run

The dog run is open all day everyday however we have an organised meet up at 12pm, 4pm and 8pm to enable the owners and dogs alike meet up and make some friends. The dog run has many fun obstacles to keep our furry friends entertained and give them a good workout!
